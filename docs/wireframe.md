# HUSA Basketball Wireframe Description

## 1. Top Navigation Bar (Sticky / Glassmorphism)
- **Left**: HUSA Logo (Shield/Vector) - Click to Home
- **Center**: Navigation Links (Home, History, News, Kids, Squad, Staff, Training, Match Center, Fans, Contact)
- **Right**: "Join the Team" (Button Primary), Social Icons (Insta/FB small), Login (Icon)

## 2. Home Page
- **Hero Section**: Full screen dynamic background (video or high-quality image slider).
    - Content: "HUSA BASKETBALL" (Large Bold Typography), "Pride of Agadir" (Slogan).
    - CTA Buttons: "Join our Academy" (Primary), "Latest Match" (Secondary/Outline).
- **Match Center Widget**: Float/Glass card overlay showing latest score (e.g., HUSA 86 - 72 FAR).
- **News Grid**: 3-column layout of latest news cards (Image + Title + Date).
- **Sponsors Scroll**: Horizontal infinite scroll of sponsor logos at footer.

## 3. Squad Page
- **Filter Bar**: Positions (Guards, Forwards, Centers).
- **Player Cards**: Portrait card, player image cut-out effect on hover.
    - Overlay: Name, Number, Position.
    - Click -> Modal with stats/bio.

## 4. Kids Reservation Page
- **Split Layout**:
    - Left: Information, pricing, age categories breakdown.
    - Right: Registration Form.
        - Steps: Child Info -> Parent contact -> Confirmation.
        - Style: Glassmorphism form container on court background.

## 5. Admin Dashboard
- **Sidebar**: Dashboard, Posts, Players, Reservations, Users, Settings.
- **Main Area**:
    - Stats Overview (Total reservations, active players).
    - Quick Actions (Add Post, Add Result).
    - Tables: Data tables for reservations/applications with "Export Excel" button.

## 6. Footer
- **Columns**: Quick Links, Contact Info, Newsletter Signup.
- **Bottom**: Copyright & Credits.
- **Style**: Dark background, simple grey text.
